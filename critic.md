# Our observations about Takaful Insurance's Website

- The website was overall interactive
- We particularly liked how the hovering when selecting of options was especially visible alongside other graphical representations of selections like icons vibrating.
- The website navigation was seamless and proved to be useable by any demographic of user.
- The website represented the organization's information very well and concisely, information including the contacts to the organizations, what the organisation is about and what type of services they offer.

## Our criticisms 

- Nothing is particularly explained when it comes to the "Mnara" insurance in the website, furthermore, when the link to the mnara page is clicked it leads nowhere.
- The board of directors page only has an error description page when clicked offering zero information to the user clicking it.
- No possibility of chatting with the chatbot 

### Final remarks

- The website serves its general purpose well.
- Its impresive when it comes to its interactiity, GUI design and its ability to capture user attention, its colour scheme is bright and representative of what transparency the organisation is trying to make out as their basis of business and brand.
